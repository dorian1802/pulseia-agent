### Factors influencing speed

- **Prompt complexity**: More details = more time
- **Time of day**: Servers are more loaded during the day (US hours for Runway/Luma, Chinese hours for Kling)
- **Queue**: Free plans are often queued
- **Generation mode**: Turbo is 5x faster than Alpha on Runway

## Verdict by budget

### $0 budget (free)

**Recommended choice: Kling AI**

Kling's free plan offers 66 credits per day, roughly 6 daily generations. It's the most generous free plan on the market, ideal for learning and experimenting without investing.

### $10-15/month budget

**Recommended choice: Kling Standard ($6.99) + Runway Standard ($15)**

With $15/month, get the Kling Standard plan at $6.99 and use Runway's free plan for scenes requiring Motion Brush. You maximize the number of generations while accessing the strengths of each tool.

### $30-40/month budget

**Recommended choice: Runway Pro ($35)**

Runway's Pro plan offers 2250 credits and full access to advanced features. It's the best choice for a serious creator who needs fine control and cinematic quality.

### $60/month and above budget

**Recommended choice: Runway Pro ($35) + Kling Pro ($23.99)**

Combine strengths: Runway for control and quality, Kling for volume and resolution. You get the best of both worlds for about $59/month.

## Optimizing your video generation budget

### Savings strategies

1. **Prototype on free plans**: Test your prompts on free plans before consuming paid credits
2. **Use Image-to-Video**: Starting from a reference image reduces the number of iterations needed
3. **Combine tools**: Kling for volume, Runway for final quality
4. **Prefer Turbo**: On Runway, Turbo mode consumes fewer credits and is faster
5. **Batch generation**: Prepare all your prompts at once and launch generations in parallel

## FAQ

### What is the cheapest AI video tool?

Kling AI is by far the cheapest, with a Standard plan at $6.99/month and a free plan of 66 credits/day. The cost per second of generated video is 10 to 50 times lower than Luma's.

### What is the fastest tool for generating videos?

Runway Gen-3 Turbo is the fastest, with generations in 10-20 seconds. Kling is the slowest (1-5 minutes per clip), which can be a bottleneck for workflows requiring many iterations.

### Should you choose a single tool or combine several?

Combining is often the optimal strategy. Use Kling for volume and resolution, Runway for fine control and cinematic quality. For a complete production guide, check out [Create a Professional Video with AI](/blog/creer-video-professionnelle-ia).

### Are free plans sufficient for testing?

Yes, free plans allow you to test each tool. Kling is the most generous (66 credits/day), followed by Luma (30 generations/month) and Runway (125 credits one-time). Test all three before committing.

### Are prices likely to drop soon?

The trend is toward lower prices and increased features. Competition between American and Chinese players is pushing prices down. It's likely that free plans will become more generous and paid plans more competitive by the end of 2026.
